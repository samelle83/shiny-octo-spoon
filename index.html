About

Hi, I’m Sam — a marketing professional with over 15 years’ experience helping businesses turn complex ideas into clear, effective communication.

I came into marketing with a simple goal: to make life easier for marketers and stakeholders by simplifying what’s complicated and finding practical solutions that work. I enjoy the technical side — connecting systems, streamlining processes, and helping teams get the most from their tools, particularly HubSpot and other CMS platforms.

Whether it’s building campaigns, structuring automation, or improving reporting, I like to dig into the details and create order from the chaos — always with a friendly, collaborative approach.

Work

I’ve worked with multinationals, B2B software companies, and smaller businesses alike — as an employee, freelancer, and now director of my own company. My background means I understand how marketing fits within wider business goals, and I know how to get things done across different teams, cultures, and regions.

What I offer:

  • HubSpot setup & optimisation – from CRM to automation and reporting
  • Email marketing & automation – nurture journeys, lead generation, customer and partner campaigns
  • Website management – updates, structure, and content improvements
  • Webinar setup & management – end-to-end support for live events and follow-up
  • Reporting & analytics – clear, actionable insights from your marketing activity
  • Collaboration & consultancy – working directly with your team or on contract to deliver results

    • I focus on building systems and processes that make marketing easier — so you can spend less time firefighting and more time driving results.
